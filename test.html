Our Purpose

The purpose of this website is to make people aware of the social conditions of the destinations they plan to travel to. With an emphasis on people of color, we intend to ensure a safe and culturally informing representation of various locations. However, this website should not be used as an end all be all as everyone's experiences are different. This should only be used as a prelimanary assessment of your decision to travel and create an adequate amount of cautiousness when travelling!
